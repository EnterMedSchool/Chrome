# Privacy Policy for EMS Medical Glossary

**Last Updated:** January 2026

## Summary

EMS Medical Glossary is designed with privacy as a core principle. **We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.** The extension operates entirely offline and locally on your device.

---

## Data Collection

### What We Collect

**Nothing.** This extension does not collect any data about you, your browsing history, or your usage patterns.

### What We Do NOT Collect

- ❌ Personal information (name, email, etc.)
- ❌ Browsing history
- ❌ Page content
- ❌ Medical terms you look up
- ❌ Usage statistics
- ❌ Device information
- ❌ IP addresses
- ❌ Cookies
- ❌ Analytics data

---

## Local Storage Only

The extension stores the following data **locally on your device only**:

| Data Type | Purpose | Synced? |
|-----------|---------|---------|
| Extension enabled/disabled | Remember your preference | No |
| Highlight color & style | Customize appearance | No |
| Disabled websites list | Skip sites you choose | No |
| Theme preference | Light/dark mode | No |

This data:
- Never leaves your browser
- Is not transmitted to any server
- Is not accessible to us or any third party
- Can be deleted by clearing browser extension data

---

## Network Usage

### Zero Network Requests

This extension makes **no network requests** after installation. All functionality is:

1. **Bundled with the extension** - All 336+ medical term definitions are included in the extension package
2. **Processed locally** - Term matching and highlighting happen entirely on your device
3. **No external calls** - No analytics, no tracking, no updates from remote servers

### How to Verify

You can verify this yourself:
1. Open Chrome DevTools (F12) on any page
2. Go to the Network tab
3. Filter by "ems" or watch for any requests
4. You will see **zero** requests from this extension

---

## Permissions Explained

We request only the minimum permissions necessary:

### Required Permissions

| Permission | Why We Need It |
|------------|----------------|
| `storage` | Save your preferences (color, style, etc.) locally |
| `activeTab` | Highlight terms on the current page when you click the icon |

### Optional Permissions

| Permission | Why We Request It |
|------------|-------------------|
| `scripting` | Automatically highlight on sites you choose |
| `<all_urls>` | Run on all websites (only if you enable this) |

**You control these permissions.** The extension works with just the basic permissions, and you can grant additional access only if you want automatic highlighting.
